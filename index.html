WHY WESSEX

A region built for business, at every stage.

Wessex combines outstanding landscapes and historic towns with a genuinely significant business base — from nationally important defence and life sciences organisations to a growing cluster of digital, engineering and agri-tech businesses. It's a region where ambitious companies can access expertise, talent and support without the cost or crowding of a major city.

As devolution brings new powers across Wiltshire, Somerset, Dorset and BCP, Growth Hub Wessex will bring that support together into one clear front door — but for now, each area's existing Growth Hub is ready to help.
